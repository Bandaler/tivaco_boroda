Expert Work in International Disputes

We provide independent expert opinions on business valuation, damages, and financial matters for use in:

  • International commercial arbitration (SIAC, ICC, LCIA, HKIAC, etc.)
  • Investor-state disputes (ICSID, UNCITRAL etc.)
  • Court litigation (Singapore, Australia, UK etc.)
  • Mediation and pre-dispute negotiations
  • Expert determination

We act as strategic expert advisors when appointing an expert witness is not required, or an independent second opinion is valuable – especially during a pre-dispute stage or potential buyout negotiations.
